Built for NASA Space Apps 2024. The app helps farmers analyze soil moisture and water data to optimize irrigation and improve crop yields. It's map-based - you see your location, pull up soil data for the area, and get actionable insights on water usage.

We went with React and TypeScript for the frontend with a clean map-centric interface. The blue and white design keeps things readable when you're looking at geographic data. Selected in the Top 15 teams out of 100+, which was a solid result for a weekend hackathon build.
