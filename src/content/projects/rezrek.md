RezRek is a social commerce platform for Indian streetwear and fashion. The discovery feed surfaces clothing from independent brands - tees, hoodies, jewellery - with style-based filters like Desi Aesthetic, Shaadi Season, and Indo-Western. You can browse by category across men's and women's wear, search across content and brands, and watch product videos with direct buy links.

Each brand and creator gets a profile page where they post content. Users can save items to "Inspo" boards - think Pinterest but for fashion - organized by whatever categories make sense to them (streetwear, bags, jewellery). Brands create posts with titles, descriptions, product links, vibes, and target audience tags. The whole thing is designed to feel like scrolling through a curated fashion feed rather than browsing a traditional e-commerce catalog.
