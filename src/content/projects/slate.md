Built for a Frontend UI Hackathon hosted by Outlier.ai. Slate is a note-taking app where every note gets a color - pastel pink, green, purple, blue - and the whole interface is organized around that visual system. You have notes, todos, and bookmarks in a clean sidebar, and the main area shows your content as colored cards.

The design philosophy was "where colorful ideas find their home." Next.js with TypeScript and TailwindCSS. The light, airy aesthetic with pastel tones was intentional - most note apps feel heavy and utilitarian, and I wanted something that felt pleasant to open. Won a PS5 for this one, so clearly the judges agreed.
