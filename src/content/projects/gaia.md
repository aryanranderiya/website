GAIA started as this idea of building an AI assistant that actually feels personal. Not just another chatbot - something that understands your context, remembers your preferences, and helps you think through things. The backend is Python with FastAPI, the frontend is React Native with TypeScript, and the whole thing runs across mobile and desktop.

We built a conversational interface that handles everything from daily planning to brainstorming sessions. The dark UI with blue accents was intentional - it needed to feel like a tool you'd actually want open all day. It's still evolving, and the codebase has since moved to a private repo as we iterate on the next version.
