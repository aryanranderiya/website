Led a team at the New India Vibrant Hackathon 2023 to build this. The problem was simple - taking attendance in classrooms is tedious and error-prone. Our solution uses the university's Wi-Fi BSSID and unique device hardware IDs to verify that students are actually in the classroom before marking them present.

The app has separate flows for students and faculty. Students log in and submit attendance when connected to the right Wi-Fi network. Faculty members can start and stop attendance sessions and export the data to Excel. Built in Java with Firebase. The four-screen flow covers login, attendance submission, reporting, and session management - nothing extra, just what's needed.
