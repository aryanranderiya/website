Built for the Odoo Winter 2025 Hackathon with team WinPaglu. It's a complete expense management system - dashboard with expense overview, tracking, role-based access control for admins, category-based organization, and company-level management.

The frontend is Next.js with TailwindCSS, the backend is NestJS, and the database is PostgreSQL with Prisma as the ORM. The whole thing runs in Docker for easy setup. We went with a clean separation between frontend and backend, with Prisma handling migrations and seeding.
